Carolina Dreamers Car Club

Meet up. Have fun. Show cars. Bring joy.

The Carolina Dreamers Car Club (founded in 1993) is a non-profit organization formed to socialize and share experiences with other automotive enthusiasts.

"I build my weeknight plans around their events. I can get excited and meet new people every week!"

-Brian J. - financial advisor/car enthusiast

Come join the fun!

We meet every Saturday at SONIC at 10AM!
